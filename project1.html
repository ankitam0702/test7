Skincare in India

This was one of our major projects this year for our service design class. We were made to go through our daily routine and pick a problem area. It was a group project of 6 people, and we all picked skincare as our focus area since skincare is a major topic for people worldwide. It was really interesting to dive deep into this topic.

We believe that misleading advertising and unawareness of the correct usage of skincare products exists for consumers seeking better products, due to a lack of transparency and less education from brands, which leads to adverse skin reactions and long-term health risks.

Our solution was a skincare service. We have a shop in which we have colour coded products that indicate what ingredients are used in it. We do not make any products, we only sell them. We have a kiosk which a person can check out while in the shop and estheticians to help them understand what each product is about. We have a helpline number for enquiries and regular check up calls and also have a website which sells colour coded products!
