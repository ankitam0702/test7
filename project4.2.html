Fuzzy Friends

This was a game design project. It was a group of 2 and we decided to take the famous game ‘QS Watermelon’ as inspiration and we made our own cat version of the game. We designed the cats ourselves and named them. Created tutorials, rewards, challenges etc. All these things were not there in the original game, these are our additions.
